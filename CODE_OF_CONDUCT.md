# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free environment for all contributors and community members.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and professional in all interactions
- Welcome diverse perspectives and constructive feedback
- Focus on what is best for the community
- Show empathy and kindness toward others

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not tolerated:

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Trolling, intimidation, or bullying
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Spam or off-topic disruption
- Any form of discrimination based on identity

## Reporting

If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior, please report it to:

**conduct@universalmanifest.net**

Include relevant context and details to help us address the issue.

## Enforcement

Maintainers will review reports and take appropriate action, which may include:

- Removal of comments or content
- Temporary or permanent bans from contribution
- Escalation to relevant authorities if necessary
